Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a restaurant halal in Portugal?

Halal restaurants in Portugal must serve only halal-certified meat and avoid alcohol and pork products.

How can I verify if a restaurant is truly halal?

Look for halal certification from recognized Islamic organizations and check online reviews from Muslim customers.
